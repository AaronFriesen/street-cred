Silvermoon City

Important Zones

Large amounts of Silvermoon City reputation can be gained in the following zones:

  • Eversong Woods
  • Ghostlands
  • Hellfire Peninsula
  • Blasted Lands
  • Desolace

There are also daily quests available from the Argent Tournament (requires level 80), as well as Silvermoon Writ of Commendation from the Molten Front (requires level 85).

Important People

The Silvermoon City quartermaster, Magistrix Nizara, is located in Eversong Woods. At all reputation levels, she sells the Silvermoon City Tabard, which grants reputation in all dungeons.

Rewards

When Revered you have access to the following items as a blood elf:

  • Silvermoon Satchel

When Exhalted you have access to the following items as a blood elf:

  • Mantle of Silvermoon
  • Cape of Silvermoon
  • Shroud of Silvermoon
Mounts

Non-Blood Elves are able to purchase a Hawkstrider when they have reached Exalted
