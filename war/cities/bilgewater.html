Bilgewater Cartel

Overview

Bilgewater Harbor, home of the Bilgewater Cartel

The Bilgewater Cartel is a Goblin run trade cartel, operating out of Bilgewater Harbor in Azshara. It is one of the six major factions of the Horde and a player must achieve exalted reputation with the city in order to recieve the title <Ambassador of the Horde>.

Important People / Places

Important Zones:

Large amounts of Bilgewater Cartel reputation can be gained in the following zones:

  • Azshara
  • Felwood
  • Kezan
  • Tanaris
  • The Cape of Stranglethorn
  • The Lost Isles
  • Thousand Needles
  • Twilight Highlands

Important People:

The Bilgewater Cartel quartermaster, Frizzo Villamar, is located in the center of Orgrimmar. At all reputation levels, he sells the Bilgewater Cartel Tabard, which grants reputation in all dungeons.

Rewards

Items:

When Revered you have access to the following items:

  • Bilgewater Satchel

When Exhalted you have access to the following items:

  • Bilgewater Mantle
  • Bilgewater Cape
  • Bilgewater Shroud

A Goblin Trike

Mounts:

Non-Goblins are able to purchase a Trike when they have reached Exalted.

Trikes are sold by Kall Worthaton in Sen'jin Village.
