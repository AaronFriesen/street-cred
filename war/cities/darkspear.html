Darkspear Trolls

Overview

Sen'jin Village, home of the Darkspear Trolls

The Darkspear Trolls are a tribe of Trolls allied with the Horde. This faction is located both in Orgrimmar and the Echo Isles. It is one of the six major factions of the Horde and a player must achieve exalted reputation with the city in order to recieve the title <Ambassador of the Horde>.

Important People / Places

Important Zones:

Large amounts of Darkspear Trolls reputation can be gained in the following zones:

  • Stuff
  • Durotar
  • Echo Isles
  • Feralas
  • Northern Stranglethorn
  • Zangarmarsh

Important People:

The Darkspear quartermaster, Champion Uru'zin, is located in the center of Orgrimmar. At all reputation levels, he sells the Darkspear Tabard, which grants reputation in all dungeons.

Rewards

Items:

When Revered you have access to the following items:

  • Darkspear Satchel

When Exhalted you have access to the following items:

  • Darkspear Mantle
  • Darkspear Cape
  • Darkspear Shroud

A Emerald Raptor

Mounts:

Non-Troll are able to purchase a Raptor when they have reached Exalted.

Raptors are sold by Zjolnir in Sen'jin Village.
