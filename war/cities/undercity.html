Undercity

Overview

Undercity

Undercity is the capital of the Undead, located in Tirisfal Glades, at the northern edge of the Eastern Kingdoms. It is one of the six major factions of the Horde and a player must achieve exalted reputation with the city in order to recieve the title <Ambassador of the Horde>.

Important People / Places

Important Zones:

Large amounts of Undercity reputation can be gained in the following zones:

  • Arathi Highlands
  • Hillsbrad Foothills
  • Ruins of Gilneas
  • Silverpine Forest
  • The Hinterlands
  • Tirisfal Glades
  • Western Plaguelands

Important People:

The Undercity quartermaster, Captain Donald Adams, is located in the center of Undercity. At all reputation levels, he sells the Undercity Tabard, which grants reputation in all dungeons.

Rewards

Items:

When Revered you have access to the following items:

  • Undercity Satchel

When Exhalted you have access to the following items:

  • Mantle of Undercity
  • Cape of Undercity
  • Shroud of Undercity

A Brown Skeletal Horse

Mounts:

Non-Forsaken are able to purchase a Skeletal Horse when they have reached Exalted.

Skeletal Horses are sold by Zachariah Post near Brill in Tirisfal Glades.
