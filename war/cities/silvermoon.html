Silvermoon City

Overview

Silvermoon City

Silvermoon City is the capital of the Blood Elves, located in the northeastern part of Eversong Woods within the kingdom of Quel'Thalas. It is one of the six major factions of the Horde and a player must achieve exalted reputation with the city in order to recieve the title <Ambassador of the Horde>.

Important People / Places

Important Zones:

Large amounts of Silvermoon City reputation can be gained in the following zones:

  • Eversong Woods
  • Ghostlands
  • Hellfire Peninsula
  • Blasted Lands
  • Desolace

Important People:

The Silvermoon City quartermaster, Magistrix Nizara, is located in Eversong Woods. At all reputation levels, she sells the Silvermoon City Tabard, which grants reputation in all dungeons.

Rewards

Items:

When Revered you have access to the following items:

  • Silvermoon Satchel

When Exhalted you have access to the following items:

  • Mantle of Silvermoon
  • Cape of Silvermoon
  • Shroud of Silvermoon

A Red Hawkstrider

Mounts:

Non-Blood Elves are able to purchase a Hawkstrider when they have reached Exalted.

Hawkstriders are sold by Winaestra at the exterior stables south of Silvermoon City in the Eastern Kingdoms.
